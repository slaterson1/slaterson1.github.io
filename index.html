Experience

My journey began in the medical field as an Anesthesia Technician troubleshooting, repairing, and maintaining anesthesia equipment. As I was exposed more and more to the software side of medicine my interest in code began to grow. I was led to the Iron Yard in Atlanta and their 12-week, immersive coding program focusing on Ruby on Rails. In this program we began with the basics of Ruby and moved progressively through HTTP, Postgres databases, designing RESTful API's, Rails, and so much more.

Projects

Inside the Park

What is more quintessentially American than Baseball and Roadtrips? Inside the Park allows you to plan your dream Baseball themed roadtrip. Integrating several API's from SeatGeek, Google Maps and SkyScanner, Inside the Park brings the roadtrip of a lifetime to your fingertips. Check out the GitHub repo here.

Bongo

Bongo is a beautiful, simple, responsive game where you are presented with a high resolution photo and you must guess where in the world it represents. The game keeps score based on time taken for guesses as well as right/wrong guesses. There is also a leaderboard for, you know, bragging rights! Check out the GitHub repo here.

Voter

The Iron Yard and Soltech partnered to host a weekend long hackathon. I worked in a team of 2 front-end developers and one other back-end developer. The app we created, Voter, is a proof-of-concept into the future of voting. It not only gives real-time voting results in both your district and around the country, it also allows for secure and anonymous voting. Check out the GitHub repo here.
