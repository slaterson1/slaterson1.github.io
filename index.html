Applications

Inside the Park

What is more quintessentially American than Baseball and Road trips? Inside the Park allows you to plan your dream Baseball themed roadtrip. Integrating several API's from SeatGeek, Google Maps and SkyScanner, Inside the Park brings the roadtrip of a lifetime to your fingertips. Check out the GitHub repo here.

Bongo

Bongo is a beautiful, simple, responsive game where you are presented with a high resolution photo and you must guess where in the world it represents. The game keeps score based on time taken for guesses as well as right/wrong guesses. There is also a leaderboard for, you know, bragging rights! Check out the GitHub repo here.

Voter

I worked in a team of 2 front-end developers and one other back-end developer for a hackathon. The app we created, Voter, is a proof-of-concept into the future of voting. It not only gives real-time voting results in both your district and around the country, it also allows for secure and anonymous voting. Check out the GitHub repo here.
